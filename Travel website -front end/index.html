Mount Lavania Hotel

The Mount Lavinia Hotel Colombo is a historic beachfront hotel in Colombo, Sri Lanka, offering a blend of colonial-era charm and modern comfort. With its rich heritage dating back to the 1800s, stunning ocean views, and serene atmosphere, it's a top choice for travelers seeking a unique and relaxing experience. The hotel provides comfortable accommodations, delightful dining choices, and direct access to a private beach, making it an ideal destination for both leisure and exploration of Colombo's vibrant culture. Enjoy a stay filled with luxury and history at the Mount Lavinia Hotel.

Gal Oya Lodge

Gal Oya Lodge is a secluded eco-retreat nestled in Sri Lanka's Gal Oya National Park. With rustic-chic accommodations, eco-friendly practices, and close proximity to untouched wilderness, it's a haven for nature lovers and adventurers. Here, you can embark on wildlife safaris, serene boat rides on Senanayake Samudraya lake, and engage with indigenous communities for cultural experiences. Gal Oya Lodge offers a tranquil escape to reconnect with nature while promoting sustainability and responsible tourism.

Gal Oya National Park,SriLanka
